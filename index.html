What types of flooring products does Smart Floorings offer? +

We offer a range of flooring options, including E500 7mm Raised Disc, E500 7mm Tiles Smooth, ESD/Anti-Static Flooring, R500 Recycled Floor Tile, customizable tiles, and more, catering to various industrial needs with durability and longevity.

What are the key benefits of using your products in industrial settings? +

Smart Flooring products offer superior durability, resistance to chemicals, abrasions, and heavy foot traffic, making them ideal for industrial spaces that require high-performance flooring solutions.

What are ecotiles made of? +

Ecotiles or eco-friendly tiles are made from various sustainable materials and processes. Most eco tiles are made of recycled materials, including discarded glass, ceramics, or even plastic waste. They get a second life as ecotiles, reducing landfill burdens and resource demands. Ecotiles are also made of renewable resources like cork, bamboo, reclaimed wood, or responsibly harvested clay, contributing to a greener footprint.

How does Smart Floorings support sustainability efforts in industries? +

Our R500 Recycled Floor Tile is an eco-friendly option that utilizes recycled materials, contributing to sustainability while maintaining durability and performance in industrial spaces.

How is tile eco-friendly? +

Tiles can be environmentally friendly in a number of ways. Several tiles are manufactured using energy-efficient methods, resulting in reduced emissions during production. High-quality tiles often require minimal cleaning with water. These tiles can also last for decades, minimizing replacements and waste generation. Ecotiles, which are often manufactured from recyclable materials, can be recycled at the end of their lifespan.

How do your flooring solutions contribute to employee well-being in industrial settings? +

Our impact-resistant and ergonomic tile design reduces the risk of injuries and fatigue, promoting employee well-being and productivity in physically demanding work environments.

Are tiles good for the environment? +

Some tiles, like ecotiles, are good for the environment as they are manufactured from recycled materials and sustainably sourced resources. Ecotiles are durable enough to last for decades and can easily be recycled at the end of their lives. However, traditional tiles are manufactured using materials and processes that can potentially harm the environment.

What are the advantages of rubber mats? +

Rubber mats offer excellent durability, slip resistance, and shock absorption. They are commonly used in gyms, playgrounds, and industrial settings to provide safety, comfort, and protection.

What should I consider when choosing commercial-grade Flooring? +

Factors to consider include durability, ease of maintenance, slip resistance, fire resistance, and the specific needs of the commercial space, such as high foot traffic or specialized requirements.

What industries does Smart Floorings cater to? +

We provide solutions for a wide range of industries, including manufacturing, automotive, healthcare, retail, education, sports facilities, and more, offering durable and practical flooring options.
